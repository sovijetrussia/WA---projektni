About GPU4U

GPU4U is tech news company which specializes in graphics card comparison

On our page you can find most of the details that you want to know before or after you purchase a new card for yourself or anyone you care about.

We will help you choose between whats most important for you, budget or just pure performance. Based on some graphs you will be able to decide do you want to go for a team green or team red, with team green being nVidia and team red being AMD. Our data comes from very reliably sources but most of the testings we do ourselves when companies decide to send us review samples of their products.

We also cooperate with Techpowerup, GamersNexus, JayzTwoCents and Tom's Hardware so you can find links to their sites on here as well.
